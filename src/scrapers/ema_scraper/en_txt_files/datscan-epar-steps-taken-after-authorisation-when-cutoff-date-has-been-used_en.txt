Procedural steps taken after granting the Marketing Authorisation 
For procedures finalised after 1 February 2004 please refer to module 8B. 
• 
On 7 August 2000, the Marketing Authorisation Holder submitted to the EMEA an application 
(II/1) for a Type II variation to add another presentation, i.e. a vial containing 5 ml solution in 
addition to the 2.5  ml vial,  which was  originally  authorised  on  27  July  2000.  The  opportunity 
was also taken to revise some of the preclinical safety margins defined in the original SPC. A 
positive  CPMP  opinion  was  given  on  19  October  2000,  and  a  Commission  Decision  with 
revised annexes was issued on 22 February 2001
